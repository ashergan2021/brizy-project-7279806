Why we started

Children who want to bring their ideas to life are limited by:
 
• Lack of money to build their prototypes
• Lack of knowledge on electronics, construction or coding
• No access to expensive tools and machinery

Current prototyping techniques are extremely wasteful, and are often thrown away/unused after completion

Our Vision

Create a STEAM Education kit that is
 
• Affordable and accessible
• Intuitive and simple to understand
• Hands-on learning experience
• Low skill floor and high skill ceiling
Instills sustainable habits​
​
Most importantly enabling young innovators to bring their ideas to life.

Anyone with a 3D printer can print their own set, regardless of location or budget

S.T.E.A.M stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics!
